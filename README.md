### Hackatons
I love participating in these events, with a satisfactory participation in all of them.
<br>
Due to our technical knowledge, two colleagues from the UAQ Faculty of Computer Science and I were among the 50 software development students selected to participate in the "1st HackaFest" held at the IBERO Puebla University in October 2019, by Sparkassen-Finanzgruppe (Financial Group of German Savings Banks).
<br>
On this one week event we created a project for which we were invited by Sparkassen to the FIRA (Trusts Instituted in Relation to Agriculture) Symposium called "New Technologies and Financial Inclusion" in the city of Morelia in November 2019.
<br>
On this symposium we showcase technological innovation solutions proposed for the exponential growth of support that the Savings Banks provided to the Mexican countryside.
